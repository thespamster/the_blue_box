Welcome to the Blue Box Exchange

This is an e-commerce site for all things Dr. Who related. As a collector myself I have enjoyed building my collection over many years.

This site has been designed to offer collectors a place to buy and sell Who related merchandise. All of the financial side is handled by Shopify so is safe and secure.

You can browse the site without creating an account. Account creation is free and allows you to buy/sell, use the message boards and post artwork to the fan art section.

Please check out the rules below. Enjoy the site and happy collecting.

Rules

The rules are very simple: Be nice and respect each other!

We want to encourage a friendly place to discuss our favourite show.

This site is a place to celebrate all the differences that make Whovians a great fanbase.

It is absolutely fine to have a different opinion to other people posting here.

However. Any abusive behaviour will not be tolerated and can potentially result in a ban.
